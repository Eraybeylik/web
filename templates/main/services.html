Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and the immigration process.

How long does the application process take?

Processing times vary by country and permit type, typically ranging from 2-8 months. We provide realistic timelines during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What documents do I need to prepare?

Required documents vary based on your specific situation and destination country. During consultation, we provide a comprehensive checklist tailored to your application type.

Do you guarantee application approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval (as final decisions rest with immigration authorities), our expert preparation and 95% success rate demonstrate our commitment to maximizing your chances of success.

Can you help with family members' applications?

Yes, we provide comprehensive family immigration services, including spouse visas, dependent children permits, and family reunification programs.
